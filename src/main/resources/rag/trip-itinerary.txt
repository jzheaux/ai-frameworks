# San Fransisco School Trip Itinerary

Below is the itinerary for our high school band’s trip to San Francisco, staying at **The Inn at Union Square**! 
Each morning is dedicated to rehearsal in preparation for **Friday night** and **Saturday (matinee & evening)** performances. 
You’ll see two organized group activities on **Tuesday** and **Thursday** afternoons—these are special opportunities you likely couldn’t organize on your own. 

**Note:**  
- Every day, **12:00 PM – 1:00 PM** is free for lunch.  
- Every day **from dinner onward** is free time **unless** there’s a scheduled performance that evening.  
- Curfew and return to hotel is typically **10:00 PM**, except on performance nights (see below for details).

---

## **Day 1 (Sunday) — Arrival**  
- **3:00 PM** – Check-in at The Inn at Union Square  
- **3:00 PM – 6:00 PM** – Unpack, settle in, short neighborhood walk if desired (in groups, with chaperones)  
- **6:00 PM – 7:00 PM** – Dinner (on your own, nearby restaurants/cafés)  
- **7:00 PM – 10:00 PM** – Free time  
  - Explore Union Square, relax at hotel, or small group activities with chaperone approval  
- **10:00 PM** – Curfew (in hotel rooms)

---

## **Day 2 (Monday) — Rehearsals Begin**  
- **7:00 AM – 8:00 AM** – Breakfast at hotel (or nearby)  
- **8:00 AM – 12:00 PM** – Band rehearsal (hotel conference room or rented rehearsal space)  
- **12:00 PM – 1:00 PM** – **Lunch (free time)**  
- **1:00 PM – 5:00 PM** – Free time / Optional afternoon activities  
  - Suggested: Explore local landmarks on foot (Chinatown, Ferry Building), or rest  
- **5:00 PM onward** – Dinner (on your own) and evening free  
- **10:00 PM** – Curfew

---

## **Day 3 (Tuesday) — Group Activity #1**  
- **7:00 AM – 8:00 AM** – Breakfast  
- **8:00 AM – 12:00 PM** – Rehearsal  
- **12:00 PM – 1:00 PM** – **Lunch (free time)**  
- **1:00 PM – 5:00 PM** – **Group Activity #1: Musical Workshop & Backstage Tour**  
  - **Example**: A private workshop at the San Francisco Opera or SF Symphony, including a backstage tour. Transportation provided.  
  - This is an exclusive opportunity to learn from professionals and see behind the scenes!  
- **5:00 PM onward** – Dinner (on your own) and evening free  
- **10:00 PM** – Curfew

---

## **Day 4 (Wednesday) — Midweek Rehearsals**  
- **7:00 AM – 8:00 AM** – Breakfast  
- **8:00 AM – 12:00 PM** – Rehearsal  
- **12:00 PM – 1:00 PM** – **Lunch (free time)**  
- **1:00 PM – 5:00 PM** – Free time  
  - Possible excursion: Walk to SFMOMA (about 10 min) or shop in Union Square  
- **5:00 PM onward** – Dinner (on your own) and evening free  
- **10:00 PM** – Curfew

---

## **Day 5 (Thursday) — Group Activity #2**  
- **7:00 AM – 8:00 AM** – Breakfast  
- **8:00 AM – 12:00 PM** – Rehearsal  
- **12:00 PM – 1:00 PM** – **Lunch (free time)**  
- **1:00 PM – 5:00 PM** – **Group Activity #2: Team-Building & Masterclass**  
  - **Example**: A masterclass at a professional recording studio or a team-building ropes course (outside the city, bus transportation provided).  
  - Focus on building ensemble cohesion and learning new techniques.  
- **5:00 PM onward** – Dinner (on your own) and evening free  
- **10:00 PM** – Curfew

---

## **Day 6 (Friday) — Performance Night**  
- **7:00 AM – 8:00 AM** – Breakfast  
- **8:00 AM – 12:00 PM** – Final morning rehearsal  
- **12:00 PM – 1:00 PM** – **Lunch (free time)**  
- **1:00 PM – 3:00 PM** – Dress rehearsal or final run-through (at performance venue)  
- **3:00 PM – 5:00 PM** – Free time (rest, light sightseeing)  
- **5:00 PM – 6:00 PM** – Dinner (on your own, but stay near venue)  
- **6:00 PM** – Call time at venue  
- **7:00 PM – 9:00 PM** – **Evening Performance #1**  
- **9:00 PM – 9:30 PM** – Pack up / transition  
- **9:30 PM – 10:00 PM** – Return to hotel (group travel)  
- **10:00 PM** – Curfew

---

## **Day 7 (Saturday) — Two Performances (Matinee & Evening)**  
- **7:00 AM – 8:00 AM** – Breakfast  
- **8:00 AM – 9:30 AM** – Warm-up / brief rehearsal  
- **9:30 AM – 11:00 AM** – Free time  
- **11:00 AM** – Call time for matinee performance  
- **12:00 PM – 2:00 PM** – **Matinee Performance**  
- **2:00 PM – 5:00 PM** – Break and free time (be mindful of performance schedule)  
  - Grab a quick lunch/late snack  
- **5:00 PM** – Call time for evening performance  
- **6:00 PM – 8:00 PM** – **Evening Performance**  
- **8:00 PM – 9:00 PM** – Wrap-up, final applause, pack instruments  
- **9:00 PM – 9:30 PM** – Return to hotel as a group  
- **9:30 PM – 10:00 PM** – Quick debrief or celebration in hotel lobby (if arranged)  
- **10:00 PM** – Curfew

---

## **Day 8 (Sunday) — Departure**  
- **7:00 AM – 8:00 AM** – Breakfast  
- **8:00 AM – 9:00 AM** – Pack up, check out of hotel  
- **9:00 AM onward** – Depart San Francisco (travel day)

---

### **General Tips & Reminders**  
1. **Stay in Groups & Follow Chaperones’ Instructions**  
2. **Be Timely:** Ensure you arrive **on time** for rehearsals and performances.  
3. **Meals:** Except for group activities, lunch is always on your own between **12:00 PM – 1:00 PM**.  
4. **Evening Free Time:** Any day without a performance, you’re free after dinner—but remember the **10:00 PM** curfew.  
5. **Represent Our School:** You’re ambassadors of our band and school. Keep it classy and courteous!

Enjoy San Francisco’s culture, food, and iconic sights while perfecting your craft and shining on stage! Have a fantastic trip.
